Beverages

With long transport routes and ever-increasing customer demands, the beverage industry must not only offer products with an attractive design but also the highest functionality. The BASIKORE supports you in protecting your brands and tracking your goods along the entire production process using logistics technologies.

Outstanding performance from start to finish

Challenges in the area of beverages include demand for special resistance, high durability and long service life in a wide range of products. The quality standards of the BASIKORE ensure the exceptional firmness of labels and holograms and offer your goods the maximum variety of finishes. Our products promise comprehensive protection against counterfeiting and theft with high-quality finishing and maximum durability. The holograms can withstand any extreme requirements – they are moisture-resistant, washable and suitable for freezing. In accordance with food law regulations, the BASIKORE complies with strict legal requirements and uses only low-migration and food safe paints, lacquers and adhesive materials in this field.

Our special labels offer maximum protection against counterfeiting and 100% control thanks to continuous traceability. The Hologram Company guarantees the use of ph-neutral and ski sensitive production materials using state-of-the-art production techniques. We guarantee you top quality, ensuring the greatest possible satisfaction of your end consumers. Our products impress with their efficiency, regardless of the product content, and provide your goods with a special level of security.
