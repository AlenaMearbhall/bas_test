Custom SBE Ultra-thin Tamper Evident Security Seal

  • Is made of only glues and inks
  • Tearing-off is immediately visible
  • Ideal for paper documents
  • Ultra-efficient against frauds
Format
40x15mm
50x20mm
60x25mm
Description

This highly secure ultra-fine anti-tampering SBE security seal is an exceptional tool that effectively protects your confidential documents, ensures authenticity of your property, by preventing any tampering attempts. The four security points make the seal non-reproducible, non-transferable, peel-resistant and customizable.

This seal is ideally used by public or private companies, private or government institutions that need to ensure a good level of security of the goods, documents or couriers that transit.

Once affixed on a document or object such as a drawer or a safe door, the seal cannot be removed without leaving traces, which immediately warns of an attempt at fraud.

A seal with reinforced security with 4 possible security points

1- A strong breakable adhesive that firmly sticks to all types of surfaces and is not removable. The technical design used allows the seal to suffer no damage and remain intact in difficult situations such as direct contact with water or solvents.

2- The absence of support between the glue and the inks is a safety point ensuring the nontransferability of the seal. This combination of the two processes makes the seal peel-resistant in one piece. It frays, immediately revealing the attempt of fraud.

3-The reactive UV inks (optional) allow you to put your logo, that appears in luminescent red, only when the seal is exposed under a UV lamp. Totally invisible under ambient light, this security point guarantees the authenticity of the goods you wish to seal.

4- Unique authenticator (according to design): A white space on the security seal (depending on your model) will allow you to display the text of your choice, such as a number, code, date, or signature, using a permanent pen in order to make the seal unique.

A seal that deters fraud

The technology used for this seal, which directly connects a very powerful adhesive and inks, automatically leaves visible traces if one tries to remove the seal that has been previously placed on a document or object. Thus, the seal dissuades anyone from fraud.

A security seal that clearly reveals the tampering. The security seal can be used in many situations.

Firstly, the security seal can be used to prove the authenticity of a document. It can be a paper, an envelope, a box, a file or a booklet. Thanks to your logos visible only under a UV lamp, the seal is nonreproducible and provides a real proof of the support’s authenticity. Secondly, the security seal can be used to prove the non-tampering of a box, a door (safe or vehicle) or a drawer. You will be aware of an attempted fraud as soon as the seal is frayed.

A customizable anti-tampering seal in the colors of your company

To customized this anti-tampering ultra-fine seal, you can enter fixed information such as texts and / or logos in the image of your company. Different types of models are possible depending upon the utility and safety required.

To make your seal, you can select up to 3 standard colors available for this product.

Related to an industrial decal, this adhesive seal sticks very easily. All you have to do is remove the protection from the adhesive, place the seal on the desired surface by pressing hard, then remove the upper layer of the protection.

For more security, you can add a transparent varnish to your anti-tamper seal in order to protect the surface. The transparent color is not counted as a color.
