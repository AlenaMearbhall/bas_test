For Custom Machinery from China, Sign Non-disclosure Agreement.

If you are importing custom machinery from China, you must sign a Non-disclosure Agreement with your manufacturer. This is primarily to prevent your manufacturer from using your design specications on another machine.
An elaborate NDA should consist of the following elements:
  • Identication of the parties involved in signing the particular agreement.
  • Identify the particular specications of your machinery that should e considered condential
  • Dene the reason for not sharing the specications and the consequences of sharing the information
  • Give the timeframe for the validity of the agreement.
Depending on your manufacturer, you can have used one of the following NDAs:
Unilateral NDA
Under this NDA, either you or your manufacturer discloses the privacy information and expects the other party to prevent its further disclosure. In most cases, you are the one who shares the custom specication of your machinery with your manufacturer. It is the duty of your manufacturer not to share the information with other parties.
Bilateral NDA
In a bilateral NDA, you and your manufacturer disclose the information to each other. However, you both agree to prevent further disclosure of condential information relating to your machinery. Here, both you and your manufacturers share the specications for your custom machinery. It is then the duty of both of you to prevent further disclosure of this information.
Multilateral NDA
This type of NDA involves more than one-two parties. Dene the reason for not sharing the specications and the consequences of sharing the information. Give the timeframe for the validity of the agreement. Here, one of these parties shares condential information relating to your machinery with the other parties. The other parties then must prevent further sharing of the information.
An NDA is a legal document that you should consider when importing your custom machinery from China, especially when:
  • The use of machinery is new and has a huge potential for changing operational processes.
  • You are engaging with a new manufacturer.
  • When the investment for manufacturing the machinery is high, and you want to return your investment first.
Among the benefits you gain from signing an NDA when importing custom machinery from China in clude:
  • it shields you and your machinery from potential loss arising from sharing the confidential information.
  • The confidential information is secret, which helps you in maximizing your profit.
When creating an NDA, you need to take care of the following issues:
  • Use simple language and be precise about the confidential information you don’t want to share.
  • Include the timeframe for the lapsing of the document
  • Ensure all the involved parties understand, and you include them in the drafting of the document
