Description

The Laserlab blank metallic A4 tamper proof laser printing label not only allows you to accurately identify your goods but also protect them and discourage tearing-off. This polyester label is available in the form of laser A4 sheets, that allow you to customized them on your own: a very useful advantage when you do not know your numbering in advance.

VOID laser asset label for an effective and accurate inventory

In the three months following the completion of an inventory using standard labels, about 10% of the stock lose their labels because of shifting or removal of labels. Thanks to this VOID laser label, you can significantly reduce this error rate since it is completely non-transferable.

The diversity of our customers who have purchased these labels is indicative of their versatility and strength. You can stick these Laserlab metallic blank A4 tamper proof laser printing labels, not only on your tools, machines and industrial components, but also on your car, high-tech products, furniture and other manufactured goods.

The highly resistant laser label is very easy to use: once the sticker protector has been removed, all you have to do is apply it on the equipment that you want to inventory or seal and the laser label will fit the contours perfectly. Being non-transferable, it is impossible to restick the laser label elsewhere. This polyester laser label not only identifies but also secures your goods. In case of removal, the breakable adhesive reveals the word VOID or a checkerboard pattern not only on the surface of the equipment but also on nthe laser label, rendering the label invalid and damaged for reuse.

By using this laser label as a seal on your equipment, you can easily detect if it has been forced open, and when you use it as an inventory label you can easily identify attempts to tear-off, because the removal signs become instantly visible, thus you can replace it immediately. This VOID laser label is a real deterrent and can serve as an effective protection of guarantees.

The VOID laser label combines quality and efficiency

Designed for real situations where the secured inventories require frequent handling, this metallic polyester label is resistant to temperatures ranging from -40° to +180°, and also to oil, moisture, conventional cleaning products and UV radiation.

Made of three layers which bind together when assembled, the finish and result of this polyester laser label are impeccable.

1) The first layer is a very strong breakable VOID acrylic adhesive (metallic or white) which ensures an excellent holding strength. If torn-off, it will leave a trace not only on the label, but also on the product.

2) This adhesive latches on to a flexible surface made of transparent polyester that is resistant to moisture and common cleaning products.

3) Finally, the third layer is the surface treatment with satin material that effectively absorbs the printing inks which will eventually be embedded on this surface.
