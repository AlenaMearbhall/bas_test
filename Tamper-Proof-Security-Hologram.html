Description

A new range of high security hologram labels intended for authenticating and securing your sensitive documents and products that can be copied.

This forgery-proof holographic label is the most effective solution against counterfeiting which is a menace for a large number of brands for different activities. The CIB (Counterfeiting Intelligence Bureau) estimates that counterfeiting accounts for 10% of the global trade, that equals to about 320 billion euros. The SBE adhesive hologram addresses this problem

For what is a holographic security label used?

Advanced technological design for 4 safety points

The holographic label guarantees a high degree of security, simple and quick authentication as well as unlimited possibilities of design and security. The adhesive hologram is generally generated using an elaborate tridimensional technology that makes it difficult to falsify due to its complexity and the cost of the technology used in its design.

Such a hologram label can neither be scanned nor photographed: thus, it cannot be fraudulently duplicated which, in principle, guarantees that it is tamper proof. The difficulty to duplicate proves the product’s authenticity. The holographic label is thus used as a means of brand communication and protection.

These forgery-proof adhesive holograms are impossible to photocopy thus allowing you to authentify and secure all your important documents: mails, checks, signatures, temporary and permanent access badges, official documents...

What are the possible uses?

A holographic sticker can be used for a variety of applications. For securing packaging: A hologram can allow you to validate the authenticity of a package by proving that the interior has not been compromised. As a certificate of authenticity: These stickers can prove the authenticity of a high-tech product, such as video games or limited quantities of vinyl or CDs.

For document authentication: Holographic stickers can allow you to witness the authenticity of official documents, such as diplomas, certifications or others.

For ticketing and access badges: The sticker prevents the creation of duplicates and secures access.

Thus, holographic stickers can have many functions and be applied to many types of companies depending on your needs. Note that the stickers are fully customizable, it adapts to the use you will make of it.

What level of security does the tamperproof hologram label provide?

These holographic labels are designed using numerous specific layers that constitute their unforgivable feature. These authenticate the product. The adhesive holograms are effective against breach of security, fraud and counterfeiting.

Each type of adhesive hologram offered in this range has its specific "security points" which ensure the desired security level and the degree of control to be chosen based onyour requirements in authentication of documents and property to be secured.

Moreover, any attempt at removal will permanently destroy the adhesive hologram (thanks to the "VOID" breakable adhesive) and make evident any attempt at tampering both on the label and on the surface of the product. For enhanced authentication, each hologram label is deeply laser engraved with a unique tamper-resistant serial number (selfdestructs in case of a tampering attempt). Laser image number

How is the hologram label designed?

All our adhesive holograms are genuine 3D holograms (not just simple diffracting metallic labels), that enable product authentication mostly with the naked eye (and for certain security points by using a scanning tool) thus ensuring the genuineness of the product on which it is affixed.

There are 3 major technologies to make holograms. The range that we offer consists of "2D/3D" and "dot matrix" holograms - 2D/3D holograms manufactured using an optical laser for image recording.

- Dot-matrix hologram manufactured using laser technology controlled by a computer system. Offers finer finish, more intense light diffraction and more security points.

- E-Beam hologram producing very high quality holograms but very expensive and requiring production of very large quantities.

To know more about the techniques for manufacturing hologram labels and to better understand these security points, see our article "Understanding 3-Point Holograms"
