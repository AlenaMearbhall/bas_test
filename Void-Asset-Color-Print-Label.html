Void Asset Color Print Label

  • Immediately visible tear-off
  • Colour and UV printing available
  • Not reusable
  • Not transferable Security check
  • Reveals proof of break-in
Format
40x15mm
50x20mm
60x25mm
Description

The purpose of this color print barcode label is to make an inventory with reliable labels that effectively secure your goods, and also at a reasonable cost. Your inventory will be more thorough and also more accurate.

The polyester barcode asset tag for an effective and accurate inventory

In the three months following the completion of an inventory using standard labels, about 10% of the stock lose their labels because of shifting or removal of labels. Thanks to this customized VOID label, that can also be used as a barcode label, you will considerably reduce the error rate since it is completely non-transferable.

The diversity of our customers is indicative of the versatility of these VOID asset color print label, you can use them on your tools, machines, industrial components and also on cars, high-tech products, movable goods and other manufactured goods.

It is very easy to use this customized polyester label: once the protection of the adhesive has been removed, all you have to do is apply it on the material that you wish to inventory or seal, and the barcode label (print example) will fit the contours perfectly. Non-transferable, it is impossible to re-stick it on a different surface.

Not only does this VOID polyester label help you to identify it also secures your goods. In case of a tearing-off, the word VOID or a checkerboard pattern, will appear on the material and also on the barcode label, that will render the label invalid and unusable thereafter. By using this identification label as a seal on your equipment, you can easily detect instances of forced opening, and even if you use it as an inventory label you can easily identify attempts of tampering, because the removal signs become instantly visible, thus enabling you to immediately replace it. This customized VOID label is a real deterrent and is an effective protection of guarantees.

The VOID polyester barcode label combines quality and efficiency

Designed for real situations where the secured inventories require frequent handling, this customized polyester color print label is resistant to temperatures ranging from -40° to +180°, and also to oil, moisture, conventional cleaning products and UV radiation. Composed of three layers which bind together during assembly, the finish and result of this VOID barcode label are impeccable:

1) The first part is a very strong VOID breakable acrylic adhesive (metallic or white) that guarantees an excellent hold. It will leave a trace both on the label and on the product if it is torn-off.

2) This adhesive latches on to the flexible surface made of transparent polyester, and is resistant to moisture and conventional cleaning products.

3) Finally, the third layer is the surface treatment with satin material that effectively absorbs the label’s printing inks which will eventually be embedded on this surface.

All our prints are executed by high definition UV screen printing using an industrial inkjet printer: its dots are invisible to the naked eye and reading of the barcodes is very fine (more than 1200 dpi). Moreover, UV curing makes the ink extremely resistant to abrasion and delivers a bright rendering of colors, that can be compared to conventional silk screen printing for high quality barcode labels (Plate costs are to be expected for the first production).

A customized polyester label: barcode label, identification label, asset label, etc.

The customized label will be ideal if you require large quantities, do not hesitate to accurately estimate your quantity requirements and order a higher volume to make the most of our highly discounted bulk rates, you can thus make great saving. (View our rates by clicking on the tab "Discounted Rates", and discover our highly degressive pricing).

It is also possible for you to display variable information such as numbering, barcodes, QR codes, and also fixed information: texts and logos. You will thus enhance the brand image of your company thanks to the customized label. By using it as a barcode label, making your inventories will be easier. Choose the print of your choice, in colour or in transparent UV. UV ink is an advantage when it comes to printing information that is invisible to the naked eye. With the help of a UV lamp, you can prove that the destructible label belongs to you and thus justify the ownership of the product. This is an effective anti-fraud measure. UV ink is invisible on glossy synthetic labels, it is not visible to the naked eye, as you can see in the example below. This VOID color print polyester label is available in three different sizes: 40 x 15mm, 50 x 20mm and 60 x 25mm. Moreover, delivery is swift and is done in a week after press proof approval, blank material is also in stock.
